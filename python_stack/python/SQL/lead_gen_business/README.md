# Lead Gen Business Assignments

This folder contains SQL assignments for managing and analyzing a lead generation business database.

## Contents
- **`lead-gen-business-new.sql`**: Database structure and initial data.
- **`lead-gen-business-quries.sql`**: Business logic and analytical queries.

## Key Business Analytics
The queries in this project focus on:
- **Revenue Analysis**: Calculating total revenue for specific months or clients.
- **Lead Tracking**: Counting leads generated per site and per client within specific timeframes.
- **Inventory**: Listing websites owned by specific clients.
- **Performance Reporting**: Generating monthly reports for client leads and site performance.
- **Client Summaries**: Concatenating all sites owned by a client into a single report.
